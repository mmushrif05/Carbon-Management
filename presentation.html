How It Works
Full construction lifecycle tracking

From raw material production to on-site construction ‚Äî every gram of CO2 is captured, verified, and reported across three clear stages.

üè≠
Material Production & Manufacturing
Tracks carbon from raw material extraction through factory processing. Each material is matched to international emission factor databases (ICE DB). Contractors report actual supplier EPD values against industry baselines.
üöõ
Transport & Logistics
Auto-calculated alongside material entries. Captures delivery distance and transport mode (road, rail, sea). Accounts for the carbon cost of moving materials from factory to site.
üèóÔ∏è
On-site Construction
Separate monthly reporting for diesel fuel, grid electricity, generator hours, and water consumption on the construction site. Direct measurement of site-level emissions.
